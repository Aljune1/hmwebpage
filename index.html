About Us

Our History

Ekings Recreational Park and Camp was develop during pandemic time and was started on December 2021. It was named Ekings Over landing before but it was decided to change its name to Ekings Recreational Park and Camp. The owner of the resort was Mr. Jude D. Sanchez and he named the resort according to his father's name.

Conservation Efforts

Ekings Recreational Park and Camp in Pandan, Antique, is committed to preserving its natural environment and promoting sustainable development. Its conservation goals include maintaining the pristine beauty of its landscapes, rivers, and biodiversity, protecting endemic and endangered species, and fostering eco-friendly tourism.

Mission and Vision

To be the ultimate destination for outdoor adventure and relaxation, fostering a deep connection with nature through memorable camping experiences and thrilling activities for all ages.
